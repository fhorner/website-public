Fiona Horner


_______________________________________

I am a PhD candidate at Carnegie Mellon University studying behavioral medicine, specifically adjustment to and management of diabetes. I employ a range of observational, experimental, quantitative, and digital health methods to understand human behavior, attitudes, social interactions, relationships, and health.

As a human-centered researcher, my goal is to apply my skills in the health and technology fields to support products and systems that optimize user well-being.

My work has been supported by the National Institutes of Health, Dexcom, the American Psychological Foundation, the APA Society for Health Psychology, and Carnegie Mellon.
